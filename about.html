Welcome to Bosco Fest 2024

Beware, you’re in for a bumpy ride! Take a seat on this temporal rollercoaster, visit the haunting past, worrisome present, and unpredictable future, and try to leave your mark in the sands of history that even the relentless tides of time won’t be able to wash away. Traverse the endlessly transmogrifying labyrinth of the timeline and attempt to resolve paradoxes as elegantly as possible as the delicate scales of time lie in your hands. Build your own TARDIS or DeLorean or even Nobita’s enchanted drawer and soar through the endless skies of the timeline, exploring uncharted territories and uncovering hidden mysteries. Fighting for the sanctity of the timeline is your one and only true purpose – prepare yourself for this extraordinarily dangerous journey through time and try not to get pruned, or worse, forgotten.
