Bosco Reverie - Creative Writing

Writeup

        Get your ‘time machine’ and step into a new timeline where the past, present and
        future converge in a symphony of prose crafting. Let us embark on a creative
        writing extravaganza and get Doraemon’s ‘Anywhere Door’ to hop on a writing
        adventure, where every page turn will take you to endless possibilities. Prepare
        your wits and pick up your pens for a day of uproarious wordplay and refined
        creativity in this war of words.

Rules

        Participants: 3 (Individual)
        Duration: 60 minutes 
        1.	The participants will be provided with topics on the day of the Fest. Participants are granted the liberty to choose their 
        own subject matter.
        2.	There will be topics pertaining to 3 different languages- English, Hindi and Bengali. The schools must have 3 participants,
         each ready to compose a topic in one of the 3 different languages.
        3.	Participants are expected to write their compositions within 1000 words. 
        4.	Participants will be provided with a pen and paper. No other equipment, especially electronics, will be allowed.
        5.	The usage of dialogues and catch-phrases in the story, akin to the topic is encouraged.
        6.	The judges will evaluate the submissions on the quality of the written material, structure of the story, description,
         imagery and any other criteria they deem necessary.
        7.	Usage of vulgarity, profanity or inappropriate language will not be allowed and will lead to the submission being void.
        8.	Utilisation of unethical methods will result in immediate disqualification of the participant.
