Bosco Syncpit - Faceoff(ON STAGE)

Writeup

        Step into the inferno ring where the beats are like the ticking clock, driving
        you toward destiny. Feel the pulsating energy of the pit as your every move
        becomes a testament to skill and courage, synchronization reigning
        supreme. Amidst the hustle, aggression, and intensity shall hold the key to
        Exodus. Pinfall? or Eternal Glory? Only time will tell……

Rules

        Participants - 2

        1.	All forms of dance are allowed.
        2.	 Participants dance one by one. Opponents do not dance at the same time. 
        3.	The event will begin with a cipher where each team will have to showcase their skills for a short span of time on 
        any random track played for the first round of selection.
        4.	 This round will be followed by the selection of 8 teams (Judges are free to choose more or less as per convenience).
        5.	 Each time a team faces an opponent, there shall be 2 rounds in which both participants must exhibit their skills. 
        6.	Participants of each school dance in alternative rounds. 
        7.	Commandos are allowed in between. Commandos mean both participants of school choose to dance together in synchronisation. 
        This should not exceed 30 seconds.
        8.	 Participants are expected to be prepared to dance on any of the 20 preset songs mentioned in the email that will be sent on 29th June, 2024. 
        9.	Any obscenity or vulgarity shall not be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification and the judges’ decision is final and binding.
