Bosco Nritya - Eastern Dance

Writeup

        Channel the power of your soul through the elegant mudras, synchronize the
        ringing of your ghungroos with the apsaras and baffle the crowds of onlookers with
        your impossibly expressive and elaborate nrityas. Embrace this ancestral art form,
        whose prevalence predates the birth of language itself and with adequate fervor, the
        rhythmic tapping of feet might just be enough to invoke the favor of the legendary
        Nataraja.
