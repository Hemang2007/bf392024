Bosco Premiere - Short film

Writeup

        Lights, Camera and Action!! Bring your movie-making magic to the silver screen,
        give the audience a hair-raising, edge of the seat experience like Rambo and John
        Wick or baffle the masses with a Nolanesque thriller, making generations debate if
        the top is still spinning or not. Will you leave your beloved watchers devastated
        over Iron Man's death or hanging off the edge of a cliff wondering why Katappa
        killed Bahubali?

Rules

        Participants: 5+10
        Movie duration: 5-10 minutes approx.
        1.	All schools must register as a team, having a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 15 participants, 
        including a director, a scriptwriter, a cinematographer and an editor and the actors.
        2.	It should be of an appropriate quality, pertaining to the theme given to the schools.
        3.	The storyline of the film should be an original one and should not be plagiarised from any existing project.
        4.	Each school must give an introduction to the film, before the film starts.
        5.	The time duration of the film will include the introduction and the credits.
        6.	Exceeding the given time limit will result in penalisation.
        7.	The theme of the film for each school will be given on 26th June, 2024 and the made film must be submitted
         by 3rd July, 2024 to home.boscofest@gmail.com
        8.	No part of the film should endorse any form of vulgarity or obscenity.
        9.	The script should be predominantly in English. Vernacular phrases might be used. Excessive use of vernacular
         language as well as visual effects will lead to penalisation.
        10.	Any appropriate type of music which is available on the internet, or, self-composed, can be included occasionally.
        11.	Top 5 schools' submissions shall be screened.
