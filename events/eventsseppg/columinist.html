Bosco Columnist - Journalism

Writeup

        From Superman to Spiderman, the greatest heroes all have a journalist secret
        identity. Here, flip the tables and unveil your inner superhero as you deliver
        breaking news at warp speed. Will you grant a red-pill-like awakening or keep the
        masses indoctrinated under the propaganda of the Machines? Will you be just
        another part of the overpowering system or break free of the clutches of our AI
        Overlords - There is no such thing as bad publicity, right?

Rules

        Participants -  2
        Duration: 5 hours
        
        1.	The participants must prepare an infotainment Powerpoint Presentation of 4+1 minutes covering the entire fest. 
        2.	The participants are also required to prepare a short video of 20-40 seconds capturing the best moments of the 
            day (This can be done using any editing software and not in MS Powerpoint).
        3.	The primary language used must be English, with the option to incorporate a limited amount of vernacular language. 
            Vulgarity of any kind will not be tolerated.
        4.	The participants are allowed to bring any shooting equipment they require like DSLRs, GoPros, tripods, microphones, 
            etc. They can use background music.
        5.	No equipment shall be provided by the host school.
        6.	Loss of participants' belongings shall not be the responsibility of the host school.
        7.	Both the participants have to be present during the powerpoint screening.
        8.	Participants must submit a written report of around 150 words summarizing the fest. They must share their experience 
            and opinions through the report and can even include a short interview of another participant.
        9.	During the competition, the registered participants are allowed to interview other participants but cannot directly
            use them for help in the journalistic process, doing so will lead to immediate disqualification.
        10.	Use of unethical means will lead to immediate disqualification of the participating school.
        11.	If any material is found uploaded on social media in any form, it shall lead to immediate disqualification.
