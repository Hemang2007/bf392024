Bosco Controvert - Debate

Writeup

            “Don't raise your voice, improve your argument”
            Step into the intellectual colosseum where words wield power and arguments are
            forged in the fires of reason. Feel the tension in the air as the clock ticks down,
            driving each contender toward their ultimate destiny. In this arena, wit is your
            weapon, and logic your shield, as you battle for supremacy in the realm of
            ideologies. You must maintain your stand the way Ross did when he said, “We
            were on a break!”

Rules

            No. of participants - 2


            1.	Oxford Style of Debating will be followed for the event. 
            2.	The schools will be divided into 2 pools by a chit system, there will be one motion per pool.
            3.	A preparation time of 30 minutes will be provided for both the rounds. 
            4.	2+1 minutes of speech and 1 minute for rebuttal will be provided based on the moderator's discretion in the first round.
            5.	One participant is to speak for the motion and one participant is to speak against the motion per team. 
            6.	Six teams will qualify for the final round in which 3 teams will speak for the motion and 3 against decided as per a chit system.
            7.	3+1 minutes will be provided for speaking and 1 minute for rebuttal in the final round.
