Bosco Prototype - Model Making

Writeup

        “Wait A Minute, Doc. Are You Telling Me You Built A Time Machine...? Out of A
        DeLorean?” Here, 'Impossible' is just an excuse and 'Unthinkable' just means that
        nobody has dared to think about it yet. Split the atom, overcharge the flux capacitor
        and earn your chance to go down in history side by side with legendaries like
        Einstein and Heisenberg. Overstep the limits, bend the rules, stretch the
        imagination and you might just become the chosen one who wields the power of
        the sun in the palm of his hands.

Rules

        Participants: 4
Duration: 90 + 5 minutes
1.	Only a single electrical connection will be provided to each group. If more than one connection is required, 
     extension cords should be brought by the participants. No other resources will be provided.
2.	 Each group will get a full 90 minutes to prepare their model.
3.	 The theme for the model will be sent via email on home.boscofest@gmail.com, on 29th June, 2024 one week before the fest.
4.	 Explanation of the model should be presented by every group through Powerpoint Presentation only (laptops are to be brought
 by the participants having a stable internet connection).
5.	 The host school will not provide internet access to the participants. The Internet, if necessary, must be arranged for by the
     participants themselves.
6.	 The above Powerpoint Presentation can be prepared at home.
7.	 The dimensions of the base of the model should not exceed 4.5 ft by 1.5 ft.
8.	 Experiments involving chemical reactions (use of acids, bases, etc.) will not be allowed.
9.	 Experiments involving the use of (or with the possibility of) fire will not be permitted.
10.	 The models should be innovative and original in nature. The judges' decision shall be final and binding upon all participants.
