Bosco Jukebox - Fusion Music

Writeup

Step into this mesmerizing fusion of East and West where physical boundaries are blurred to craft international masterpieces. If Akon and Vishal Dadlani can sing Chammak Challo, Eminem can rap with Elton John, and Quickstyle can dance to 'Kala Chashma' then there's nothing to stop you from generating your own multidimensional fusion. Join us for a musical journey like no other, where diversity is celebrated and unity is found in the universal language of music.

Rules

Participants: 8+2 Duration: 6+2 minutes (including setup time) 1. Participants must perform a fusion of at least ONE song each of Eastern and Western category. 2. Vocals and instruments will both be given equal weightage during evaluation. 3. Only one drum kit with attachments will be provided by the host school, though attachments can be brought by the participants also. All other instruments are to be brought by the participants. Any type of instruments except DJ consoles and MIDI players are allowed. 4. Pre-recorded and pre-programmed music is not allowed. Laptops are permitted for use of VST tones only, but the performance must be entirely live. 5. Addition and alteration of original lyrics is not allowed. The whole song or a part of it may be used. 6. One keyboard stand will be provided to the participants. 7. Usage of objectionable or derogatory lyrics will lead to immediate disqualification. 8. It should be a uniform fusion rather than a discontinuous performance. 9. The list of selected songs with the portion of the lyrics to be performed has to be emailed to the Fest email ID (home.boscofest@gmail.com) by 29th June, 2024. The list of instruments being used by the participants and the number of singers performing must be included as well. 10. The participants are responsible for their own belongings. 11. At least 2 western and 2 eastern instruments are to be used. 12. Depiction of the theme in the performance would be appreciated.
