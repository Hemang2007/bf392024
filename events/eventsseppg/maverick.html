Bosco Maverick - Unconventional Games

Writeup

        The first rule of fight club is that… there are no rules??
        As Dante said, 'Abandon all hope ye who enter' for once you take a step into this
        hellish realm of confusion and absolute lawlessness, there is no going back. Even
        the laws of physics are scared of this vortex of madness where anything and
        everything goes. So, do you dare to gamble your sanity for a shot at glory?

Rules

        Rounds - 3
        Number of Participants - 2
        Rules:
        ● The rules shall be shared on the day of the event itself.
        ● The rounds are designed to test the coordination, intellectual prowess and
        endurance of the participants.
        ● There shall be no system of elimination in these rounds and the winner will solely
        be decided upon the summation of the 3 rounds altogether.

        Round 3 - The Inflatable Iron Lung Challenge 
        ● The final round aims to culminate the essence of endurance, stamina and coordinations altogether in this Do-Or-Die situation 
        ● Participants will be given 60 seconds and have to carry steel glasses from one table to another by means of deflated balloons!
        ● They have to blow into the balloon while keeping it inside the glass in such a way that the glass will get stuck to
         the balloon's surface due to the increased volume and maintain that while they carry it from one table to another. 
        ● The winner is the one with the most glasses in the designated table within the time limit.
